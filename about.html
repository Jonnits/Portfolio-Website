A little about me

Hi there! I'm Jon, a full-stack web developer with a passion for turning complex problems into seamless digital solutions.

Originally from the UK, I moved to California via New York about 8 years ago. Before diving into the world of tech, I spent a good chunk of my career in think tanks and business analysis, where I learned to approach problems from multiple angles and always keep an eye on the bigger picture.

I hold a bachelor's in International Politics, a master's in Global Business, and a certificate in Full-Stack Web Development from immersion training, which means I can talk code and geopolitics — though I promise I’ll stick to code here. My journey into web development has been one of discovery, growth, and lots of coffee.
